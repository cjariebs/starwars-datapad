Tatooine

Tatooine is an arid desert planet with a population of 200,000 people. Some notable residents include Luke Skywalker, C-3PO, and Darth Vader. The planet is 1% water and 99% land.

  • Diameter: 10,465km
  • Gravity: 1 standard
  • Rotational Period: 23 hours
  • Orbital Period: 304 days
